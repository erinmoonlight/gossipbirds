Viscount Augustus

A Delightful Sparrow

House Sparrows, while invasive to this continent, have produced one of our best bachelors. Viscount Augustus posesses both flash and substance, providing entertainment to all who gather near the tray. The Viscount happily shares with other birds while still ensuring his own meals are accounted for. This author expects that his careful balance of bravado and serendipity will entice many lady birds, though we do express concern over his daring behavior around cats. We hope the Viscount manages to keep himself, and his future partner, out of claw's reach. Read more about House Sparrows
